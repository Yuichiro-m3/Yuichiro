How the matching works.

Keep the experience simple for both sides: visitors explain what they want, students confirm availability, and both can chat before meeting in a public station area.

01

Visitor sends a request

Area, date, tour length, interests, language level, and travel notes are collected before introducing student guides.

02

Students receive matches

Guides are ranked by interests, neighborhood knowledge, language, and availability. The visitor can choose who feels right.

03

Meet safely in Tokyo

Meet at a major station, confirm the route, enjoy the tour, and leave reviews so future matches become more accurate.

Company information.

Campus Local Tokyo is designed as a student guide matching service that connects international visitors with trained Japanese university students in the Tokyo area.

Founded for travelers

Why the company was created.

The service was founded to make first-time Tokyo travel more human, local, and less intimidating. Instead of only offering fixed sightseeing routes, Campus Local Tokyo focuses on conversations, student perspectives, and flexible neighborhood walks.

Philosophy

Local friendship, clear trust.

Our mission is to create safe, friendly cultural exchange. Visitors should feel welcome, and student guides should be treated fairly for their time, language ability, and local knowledge.
