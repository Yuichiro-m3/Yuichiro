Move smoothly.

A few local habits make the whole trip easier, especially during train transfers, restaurant visits, and temple walks.

  1. 01 Use a transit IC card for trains, buses, lockers, and many shops.
  2. 02 Keep conversations quiet on trains and avoid phone calls onboard.
  3. 03 Carry a small bag for trash until you find a station bin.
  4. 04 Book popular restaurants early, then leave room for walk-in counters.
