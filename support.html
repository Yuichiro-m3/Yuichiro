Customer service

Support before, during, and after the tour.

GUIDE MATCH keeps the matching experience simple by making guide selection, meeting changes, safety expectations, and follow-up easy to understand.

Support policy.

Support is designed around the moments where travelers most need clarity: choosing a guide, changing a meeting point, confirming the route, understanding what is included in the price, and handling delays or cancellation requests.

For a real launch, GUIDE MATCH should keep booking records, emergency contact rules, refund conditions, identity checks for guides, and post-tour review follow-up. These steps help the service stay friendly while still feeling reliable for visitors, students, and their universities.

  • Before the tour Guide questions, route requests, price clarification, and meeting point confirmation.
  • During the tour Late arrival reports, meeting point changes, route concerns, and urgent contact guidance.
  • After the tour Reviews, refund questions, safety reports, and follow-up about the guide experience.
